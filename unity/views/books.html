Reading daily is one of the best asset that everyone can possess very easily and for free.It not only develops your imagination but also provides with a great fortune of knowledge. Reading a good book is the best thing and a great feeling that everyone should go through.It is voyage through time.You live several lives while reading.Reading helps you to build confidence,vocabulary,motivation and lot many other things. Here are some books of such books :

One of us is lying Young adult fiction, Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

If you are a fan of real life mystery and thriller this is the book that you may enjoy.Five college students are gathered in a room for detention and only four make out alive.What happened to the one?Who is the culprit?What was the masterplan?This is what this book is all about.Every single person in that room is a suspect who have their own secrets,which when revealed can keep your guesses to alter frequently and encircle you within the story making it more addictive.The unique part of this book is its writing type which is like reading a personal diary of each character.It is a pure addictive and unstoppable mystery that will make you to bang your head to solve.Is everybody in it together,or is somebody pulling the strings?Find it out on your own. Before I go to Sleep Thriller, Mystery, Suspense, Romance, Drama

What if when you woke up every morning, you could not remember anything of the day before or the day before that and you wake up beside a man who says to be your husband. A page turning and addictive psychological thriller about an amnesia woman who keeps recording all of her activities in a diary.The book starts with her unknown existence and reveals every new plot and twist as the story progresses.The book is a diary itself written by her trying to find out a new mystery each day and the unknown story of her past making it more interesting.The bits and pieces of her memories unfolds as she tries to join the missing pieces together and a terrifying truth is revealed at last giving a true justice to this book.Is her husband keeping some secrets from her or is he really trying his best to cope with her? The KingKiller Chronicle Heroic Fantasy, Magic, Love, Triology

One of the best memorable fantasy trilogy series ever.It is a story of a talented and magically gifted young man who happens to be the greatest hero the world has ever seen.Even being the most powerful and unbeatable hero,he tries to keep his identity a secret and always tries to be a normal human being in the world.But he is destined to do many things unexpectedly.The story might seem slow and quite basic at the start,but after few pages it totally takes a beautiful turn making it addictive and absolute page turner.The character development is strong and the genius narration is mind blowing with many memorable and inspiring quotes.The one sided love story is truly unique and heart touching which can even inspire your love life.Even without any intense brutal combats or heavy drama,this series does the best with its mysterious and suspenseful storyline.Give it a try and you will find an unforgettable and amazingly narrated fantasy series ever. The Stormlight Archive Fantasy, Magic, Battles, Kingdoms, Triology

The Stormlight Archive is one of the best fantasy series out in the market.Brandon Sanderson has done a great job by making this series a unique one.Unlike other fantasy worlds,except for a few imaginary things this series feels more like a fiction than fantasy.Kingdom politics,strong principles,energetic and thrilling battles are some of the extraordinary things mentioned in it.It makes you feel as if you are living in some other world making every scenario more realistic.The characters are just incredible and some of them might prove to be an ideal one in your life considering their principles and tactics.Also several quotes and sayings in this book are truly amazing and memorable which can help you in your darkest times and always keep you motivated .Enriched with beautiful world,strong character development and twisting turns and plots makes this series a one time must read. Rich Dad,Poor Dad Self-Help, Education, Finance, Investing

Who does not like to be rich or want more money? But will this happen if we still follow the traditional methods?The answer is a clear no and this is what the book is all about.A new way of thinking and reprogramming your mind about financial growth.It surely helps to be educated about the world of riches and what all are the perspectives of the riches regarding everything.The book beautifully educates through situations between two individuals where one is scholar in studies but poor in financial education while the other one is not good at studies but rich in financial education.Both have their own visions.It gracefully educates about several important things that are very crucial in our future life which are never taught in our schools,colleges or even by our family and friends.Many of the quotes and words are eye opening and surely can make you change your perspectives of life.If you want to get started for your financial education,make more money in the future and want to know about perspectives and thinking of the riches then this book is a great start.
